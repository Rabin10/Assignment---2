Travelogue: Paris

Overview

Paris is a city that feels alive on every street corner. You’ll find grand monuments and wide boulevards mixed with cozy neighborhoods that invite you to slow down and explore. Whether it’s museums, cafés, or a perfect croissant, Paris always wins you over.

Must-See Spots

  1. Eiffel Tower
  2. Louvre Museum
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral
  4. Montmartre & Sacré-Cœur
  5. Musée d’Orsay

Visitor Tips

  • Buy tickets online for popular sites.
  • Use the Métro or walk—parking is limited.
  • Learn a few basic French phrases.
  • Visit bakeries in the morning for fresh pastries.
